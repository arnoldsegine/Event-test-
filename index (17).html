Visa Guide

🇨🇦 Canadian Citizens

No visa required. Visa free for up to 90 days. Passport valid for at least 3 months beyond travel dates.

travel.gc.ca/destinations/albania

🇨🇦 Canadian Permanent Residents

Depends on your home country passport. Valid previously used US, UK or Schengen visa may qualify for visa free entry.

e-visa.al

🇺🇸 American Citizens & Green Card Holders

No visa required. US passport holders and Green Card holders are both visa free for up to one full year.

travel.state.gov

🌍 UK Visa / Schengen / USA Visa Holders

Valid previously used UK visa, Schengen permit, or USA visa qualifies for visa free entry into Albania.

e-visa.al

🇳🇬 Nigerian Citizens without a previously used UK/USA/Schengen Visa

eVisa required. 100% online application. No embassy visit needed. No in person appointment required. Apply from anywhere in the world in under 20 minutes. Your eVisa arrives by email, simply show on your phone at the border. Apply at least 30 days in advance. Fee approximately EUR 60. Use a dollar card for payment.

Apply now at e-visa.al/apply →  Full guide for Nigerians →

Visa rules can change. Always verify before booking flights.

✈️ Visas & Travel
Do Canadians need a visa for Albania? +
No! Visa free for up to 90 days. Passport must be valid for at least 3 months beyond your travel dates.
What about Canadian Permanent Residents? +
Depends on your home country passport. A valid previously used US, UK or Schengen visa may qualify. Confirm before booking.
Do American citizens or Green Card holders need a visa? +
Not at all! Both US citizens and Green Card holders are visa free for up to one full year.
I am Nigerian without a previously used UK/USA/Schengen visa. What do I need? +
You will need an eVisa. It is 100% online, no embassy visit needed. Apply at e-visa.al/apply at least 30 days before travelling. Fee is approximately EUR 60.
What airport should I fly into? +
Tirana International Airport Nene Tereza (TIA). Approximately 50 minutes to the resort by taxi.
